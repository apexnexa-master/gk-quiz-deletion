Account & Data Deletion Request

We respect your privacy and provide a straightforward way to delete your account and associated data from GK Quiz Daily.

How to Delete Your Account

Option 1: Delete from the App (Recommended)

Open the GK Quiz Daily app, go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account. Follow the on-screen confirmation to permanently delete your account and data.

Option 2: Request via Email

Send an email from the email address associated with your GK Quiz Daily account with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" to:

Send Deletion Request

We will process your request within 7 business days and send a confirmation email once completed.

Important Notes

  1. Account deletion is permanent and irreversible.
  2. You will lose all quiz history, streaks, and achievements.
  3. If you sign in again after deletion, a new account will be created.
  4. For guest (anonymous) users, clearing app data or uninstalling automatically removes all local data.
  5. If you need help, contact us at nexasoftstudios@gmail.com.
